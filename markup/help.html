Payments questions

How can I make my first investment?

Click the 'Deposit' button in the Finance section, enter desired amount, select the payment service and follow instructions

If you made a deposit via Perfect Money or Payeer, your deposit will be funded instantly.

If you made a deposit via Bitcoin, Ethereum, Litecoin, it will be funded after the transaction is confirmed.

This process can take 30 minutes or more.

Can I reinvest my profits

Sure, you can do it in your personal account.

Can I have multiple investments at the same time

Yes. You can have any amount of investments at the same time.

How long my investments will be active?

We offer unlimited investment options, which means you'll gain profit all the time while you have any amount of $ADV shares.

First of all, we are offer good reinvestment options that you can find in your personal account, but if you need to withdraw your funds, you should use Finance section of your personal account.
